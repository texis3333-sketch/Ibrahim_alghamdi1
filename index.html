Welcome

Diriyah Heritage Village is one of Riyadh's most important cultural sites. It represents the birthplace of the first Saudi state and reflects unique Najdi mud-brick architecture, narrow pathways, and welcoming gathering spaces.

  • UNESCO World Heritage Site on the edge of Riyadh.
  • Perfect for families, students, and heritage lovers.
  • Mix of restored palaces, museums, and traditional markets.
